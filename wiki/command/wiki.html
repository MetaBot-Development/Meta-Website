INFORMATION

In total, Meta has 11 public commands that server administrators, managers and other staff can user.
Meta's commands are separated into 3 different categories, moderation, information and server.

/wiki [page]

Wiki is a quick and easy way to reach this place - The Meta Wikipedia.
The wiki command shows quick links to the correct wiki document. For example, if you do /wiki [doc: setup], you will get a link for the setup command in Meta Wiki.
