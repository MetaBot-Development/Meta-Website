Scanning Your Server

Scanning as an Action

Scanning your server every now and then is very useful if you'd like to get some background information about your server.
Meta provides an advanced scanning system for free, to ensure everyone their servers safety.

Meta Scan gives you inside info about your server administrators, roles, alt accounts, members and channels. Also, Meta calculates the protection score, and gives you ideas to optimize the score.



Scanning Score Calculations

The calculation to figure out the scanning score is calculated next:

CALCULATION:

Have Meta Setup completed +15 points


Anti Mod Grief Channel Create Enabled +3 points

Anti Mod Grief Channel Delete Enabled +3 points

Anti Mod Grief Channel Modify Enabled +6 points

Anti Mod Grief Manual Ban Revoke Enabled +6 points

Anti Mod Grief Guild Update Enabled +3 points

Anti Mod Grief Dangeroul Role Given Enabled +8 points

Anti Mod Grief Role Created Enabled +3 points

Anti Mod Grief Role Deleted Enabled +3 points

Anti Mod Grief Role Modified Enabled +5 points


Anti Alt Detection System Enabled +14 points

Malicious Bot Scanning System Enabled +11 points

AutoMod Rules Used +3 points


Have less than 6 administrators +12 points

Have less than 30 suspected alt accounts in your server +5 points


Total: 100 points | 1 point = 1%

Why do we calculate it so?

We have added general point giving situations such as alt account count and administrator account count, but this is as far as we can to be 100% sure.
Why Metas scanning is so focused on Meta itself, is because we know, that if you have such things enabled, our score is not false. We rely on our own statistics and information, because we can trust this.

Maybe in the future, we will add more third party factors, but given the current situation, we'd like to provide serivce that we are sure of.
