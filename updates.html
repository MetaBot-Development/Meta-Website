New Website Launch. All you need to know!

Meta Website Relaunch

Meta has launched a brand new website update today! The goal with this site is to bring simplicity to Meta, as the last website was more for looks, this one is focused on efficiency and understandability.

We are still constantly fixing the site and brining in new updates, to ensure it's up to date and has no bugs.
Visually, we have went with a simple dark theme, more important messages are in colored boxes to ensure they are noticeable.

We understand that this might be a little more "boring" site appearances, but we ensure you, that this site is much more easier to navigate and understand. Each needed tutorial is now also in video form, in order to make everything even more simple!

Check out the navigation guide to see how this site works!
If you have any issues, make sure to let us know in out Support Server at Discord.
