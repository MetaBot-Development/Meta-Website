FAQ Accordion

How do I get started?
Simply invite the bot to your server and use /setup to configure it. The bot will guide you through the initial setup process.
What permissions does the bot need?
The bot requires Administrator permissions for full functionality, but can work with limited permissions if you only need specific features.
Is there a premium version?
Yes! Premium includes advanced features like custom branding, priority support, and extended logging.
