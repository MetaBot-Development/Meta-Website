Introduction

Welcome to the Meta documentation. Meta is a powerful Discord bot designed to provide advanced protection, automation, and control for your server.

What is Meta?

Meta is your server's ultimate guardian. Built with cutting-edge security features and intelligent automation, Meta protects your Discord community from raids, spam, malicious users, and compromised moderators. Packed with sleek and beautiful design, Meta enhances your server's functionality while maintaining top-tier protection. Whether you're running a small community or a large-scale server, Meta scales with your needs.

Key Features

Anti Mod Grief

Maximise the security of your server by preventing moderator accounts from being exploited by their privileges.

Anti Alt Detection

Automate repetitive tasks with intelligent triggers, detectors and actions. Automate kicking and detecting with one click!

Malicious App Detection

Automatically detect and block Malicious Applications that attempt to exploit your server.

AutoMod x Discord

Create detailed Discord AutoMod rules in seconds with only 1 click.

Advanced Logging System
