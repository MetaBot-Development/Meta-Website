About Meta Bot

The application that Meta is today, is around 2 years old. Frosty, the Founder of Meta, started coding a new project after the last project (Discard) was becoming too old and out of date. The app was called Geila at first and it focused on protecting your server!
Geila launched on the 29th of may 2022 and was publicly available for everyone! But on the 24th of June, 2022, everything changed. Geila was renamed to the now know Meta.

Since then, Meta has had 4 main focus points, making sure your server staff / powerful people wont ruin your server, detecting alt accounts, keeping away malicious bots and having an auto moderation system. Here at Meta, we have advertisements and other bs, so we lose money while keeping the app up, but it's worth it.
We like to provide everyone a Discord app, that is free, has no premium blockings and functions and feels awesome! Meta has a lot of designs in the bot, to keep the responses beautiful and clean.
To fund the bot, we did launch a Meta Plus, but we never take something away from free exprience, to be honest, we have implemented something for premium and then brought it to free users later... great business model am I right??

To sum it up. Meta is the Discord protection bot that works 24/7 behind the scenes, is very clean, functionable, reliable, up-to-date, verified, easy to set up and the most important thing, it's FREE. So what are you waiting for, start using Meta today!




Quick Setup Tutorial

1) Invite Meta to your Discord server.

2) Use the /setup command, choose mod-logs, admin-log channel and the Moderator rank.

3) Run the /settings command and configurate the bot all you want.

4) You're done, it was that simple!

NOTE

Only users with Administrator permissions can set / and configurate the app. General commands such as Moderation have more flexiblilty.
