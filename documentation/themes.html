How to Change Your Theme

Switching themes is quick and easy. The new theme applies instantly to all Meta messages.

Applying a Theme

Change your server's theme in seconds
1
Run /settings in your server to open Meta's configuration panel.
2
Navigate to the Appearance section in the settings menu.
3
Select your desired theme from the available options. The change takes effect immediately.

✨ Unlock Premium Themes

Nature Theme and Meta Plus Theme are exclusive to Meta Plus subscribers. Upgrade to unlock all themes and many more premium features.
